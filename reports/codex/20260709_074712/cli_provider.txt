usage: rosclaw provider [-h] {list,health,route,benchmark,invoke,diagnose} ...

positional arguments:
  {list,health,route,benchmark,invoke,diagnose}
    list                List registered providers
    health              Show provider health and capability contracts
    route               Explain provider route selection for a capability
    benchmark           Plan a provider benchmark without invoking external
                        models
    invoke              Invoke a provider capability
    diagnose            Diagnose provider interfaces against the active body

options:
  -h, --help            show this help message and exit
